Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our courses and services

What is the duration of your courses? ▼

Our standard courses are 6 months long with 3-4 classes per week. Each class is 2 hours long. We also offer accelerated 3-month intensive programs. The schedule is flexible with morning (5:00-7:00 AM) and evening (4:00-6:00 PM) batches.

Can I join mid-course? ▼

Yes, absolutely! You can join anytime during the course. We provide recorded lectures for all topics covered before your joining date. Additionally, we conduct special one-on-one doubt-solving sessions to help you catch up with the batch quickly.

What if I miss a class? ▼

All our classes are recorded and uploaded to our online portal within 24 hours. You can access these recordings anytime and watch at your own pace. We also conduct additional revision sessions for students who need extra help.

Are online classes available? ▼

Currently, we offer offline classes in Sonipat. However, we're expanding and plan to introduce live online classes soon. Students can contact us for updates about online classes.

What is your refund policy? ▼

We offer 10% refund if you withdraw within 7 days of enrollment, provided you haven't attended more than 1 class. After 7 days, fees are non-refundable but can be transferred to family members. For specific cases, contact our management.
